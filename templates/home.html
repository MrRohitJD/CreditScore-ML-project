ML Model Overview

XGBoost Classifier

Our Credit Score Prediction System leverages a tuned XGBoost Classifier trained on the Give Me Some Credit dataset (150,000 records). The model predicts whether an applicant is likely to experience serious financial distress within 2 years, with an AUC score of 0.8647.

Training Samples
Preprocessing steps: Missing value imputation (median for income, mode for dependents), feature scaling (StandardScaler), SMOTE oversampling for class imbalance, and hyperparameter tuning via GridSearchCV.
